Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our membership plans and policies.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical reasons or extended travel. A small administrative fee applies.

Is there a contract commitment?

We offer both month-to-month and annual membership options. Annual memberships come with significant savings and additional benefits.

Can I bring guests?

Premium and Elite members receive complimentary guest passes each month. Basic members can purchase day passes for guests at a discounted rate.

What if I want to cancel?

Month-to-month memberships can be cancelled with 30 days notice. Annual memberships have a 3-day cooling-off period after signup.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! We offer a 15% discount for full-time students with valid student ID. Military personnel and seniors (65+) also receive special pricing.
